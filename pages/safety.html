Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Together, we create a trusted community where everyone can rent with confidence.

Welcome to the Renturn community, where trust and safety come first

Community First

Shared Responsibility

Both owners and renters play crucial roles in maintaining safety standards across all rentals.

Agreement

By using Renturn, you agree to prioritize safety in every rental transaction.

Univesal Safety Principles

Inspection is Mandatory

Thoroughly inspect items before and after rental. Document any pre-existing damage with photos or video.

Hygiene & Cleanliness

Items must be clean and sanitized. Return them in the same condition minus normal wear and tear.

Proper Use

Use items only for their intended purpose. Read all provided instructions carefully.

Safe Environment

Use items in appropriate, safe, and dry environments suitable for the equipment.

Supervision

Never leave electrical, mechanical, or children's items unattended while in use.

Children & Pets

Exercise extreme caution. Many items are not toys and can pose serious hazards.

Category-Specific Safety Guidelines

Electronics & Appliances

Keep away from water and moisture

Check electrical cords for damage before use

Handle Li-ion batteries with care, avoid overheating

Be aware of heat generation during operation

Clothing & Textiles

Keep away from water and moisture

Avoid sharp objects that may cause snagging

Use stain protection when appropriate

Store formal wear on padded hangers

Event & Party Supplies

Respect weight limits for tables and chairs

Follow assembly instructions for structures

Properly secure all decorations

Manage cables and linens to prevent trips

Tools & Equipment

Always wear appropriate PPE (gloves, goggles, masks)

Ensure proper training before use

Properly secure work pieces

Read all safety manuals thoroughly

Emergency Situations

Injury

Seek medical attention first. Then contact owner and Renturn support.

Fire/Electrical

Power off safely if possible. Evacuate and call emergency services.

Stop using immediately. Contact Renturn to report the issue.
