Help Center

Find answers to all your questions about our rental platform

Getting Started

How do I create a new account?

Click "Sign Up", fill in your details, and verify your email to activate the account.

How do I log in?

Click "Log In" and enter your email, national ID and password.

Using the Platform

Using the Platform

Search for the product, check availability, and place a rental request.

How do I list my product for rent?

Go to "Add Product", fill in product details, set the price, and submit for approval.

How do I search and filter products?

Use the search bar and filters such as category, price, and location.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods are accepted?

Credit/debit cards and other supported online payments.

How is rental cost calculated?

Cost depends on rental duration, product price, and any additional fees.

Is there a security deposit?

Some products require a deposit to cover potential damages.

Orders & Bookings

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

You will receive a notification once your request is approved.

What do order statuses mean?

Pending = under review, Confirmed = approved, Completed = rental finished.

Returns & Pickups

How is the product picked up?

The delivery service picks it up at the agreed time and place.

What should I do when returning a product?

Follow return instructions and ensure the product is in the same condition.

Trust & Safety

How do you verify accounts?

Accounts are verified via identity and payment checks.

How can I rent safely?

Check reviews, communicate through the platform, and follow rental guidelines.

What happens if a product is damaged?

Security deposits or insurance cover damages according to our policy.

Contact Support

How can I contact support?

Use the support link / email provided on the last section "Contact Us"
